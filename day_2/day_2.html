Deciding Metrics

I want to evaluate three rating from these stats: 1. Winning rate of the team, calculated by dividing matches won with matches played. 2. Offensive rate of the team, calculated by dividing goal for with matches played. 3. Defensive rate of the team, calculated by finding the complement of goal against divided by matches played.

aff_df_metricized <- 
  aff_df_cleaned %>% 
  mutate(
    win_rate = round(w / pld, 3),
    offensive_rate = round(gf / pld, 3),
    defensive_rate = (1 - round(ga / pld, 3))
  )
